THE FALL OF AZERGATH

Large army of Orcs and Goblins is sieging the Fortess of Azergath.
As one of the Iron Brotherhood merceneries your job will be to hold
east wall against enemy onslaught. You are the leader of mercenery
veterans with rank Captain. Decisions you make could impact the
Defence of Azergath, not to mention couse your untimely death.
